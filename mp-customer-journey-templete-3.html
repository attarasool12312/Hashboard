Linear

Linear processes imply there's a set of required steps, one by one, customer should take. They can't go from A to C without B.

Non-linear time-based

Non-linear processes give customers some freedom in going from A to C - the start/end points are known, but the sequence of actions can differ.

Ongoing, non-linear

Ongoing, non-linear (or “cyclic”) processes are repeating from time to time

Bi-directional

Linear processes, where one tasks leads to another one and customer can go back to the previous step.
